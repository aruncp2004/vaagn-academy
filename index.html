What Students Will Learn & Build

Every VIEM student performs real practical activities on actual EV components and vehicles - not simulations.


Battery Pack Assembly

Assemble real lithium-ion battery packs from cells to finished pack

Battery Testing

OCV testing, IR testing, capacity testing on real battery cells

Cell Matching

Sort and match cells by voltage and internal resistance for optimal pack performance

BMS Configuration

Configure BMS parameters, protection settings and fault codes on real hardware

Controller Programming
