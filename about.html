Vaagn Institute of Electric Mobility is India's first EV training institute built inside an actual EV manufacturing facility — by the people who build commercial electric vehicles every day.

WHO WE ARE

The Institute That
Builds Real Skills

VIEM — Vaagn Institute of Electric Mobility — is the training and education arm of Vaagn Auto, a commercial EV manufacturer based in Sriperumbudur, Tamil Nadu.

We saw a gap. India's EV industry is growing at 49% CAGR — but most training institutes teach from outdated textbooks, not real vehicles. We decided to fix that.

At VIEM, students train on actual Vaagn Beast and Titan commercial EVs, inside our factory floor. You learn the same skills our own engineers use daily.

OUR STORY

Why We Started VIEM

01

The EV Skill Gap

India's EV market is exploding — but there aren't enough skilled EV technicians, engineers and service professionals to keep up. Most training is outdated and theoretical.

02

Real Training, Not Theory

As EV manufacturers, we knew what skills actually matter on the job. So we built a curriculum around real vehicles, real diagnostics and real factory processes.

03

Accessible to Everyone

From diploma students to working professionals to entrepreneurs — we designed VIEM to be affordable, flexible and relevant to anyone who wants to build an EV career.

04

Tamil Nadu First

Sriperumbudur is the heart of Tamil Nadu's automotive belt. We are proud to bring world-class EV training to this region — and eventually, to all of India.
